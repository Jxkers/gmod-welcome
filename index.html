About the Server

Welcome to Three Kings Murder — a custom Murder gamemode server focused on balanced gameplay, active moderation, and a friendly community.

Our goal is to provide a fun and fair experience for both new and veteran players, with minimal toxicity and consistent rule enforcement.

Whether you’re here to survive, deceive, or just mess around with friends, we hope you enjoy your time and stick around.

Thanks for playing — have fun and play fair. Make sure to read the rules!
